About Sanskriti Homestay

Sanskriti home - Pozhikkara near Paravoor, which is 13 km from the world famous Varkala beach is a serene, sleepy village with its beautiful, non-polluted, pristine beaches, lush greenery, with beautiful Paravoor lake in the back drop, is ideally your affordable escape option. The homestay is a traditional Kerala home with its bedrooms, living space, modern kitchen, toilets, offering you peaceful living away from all the stresses of modern living.

How To Reach

Sanskriti home is just 53.2 km away from Thiruvananthapuram International airport, 3.3 km to Paravoor Railway station, 12.5 km to Kollam Railway station and 14.1 km to NH66. The homestay is on the Varkala - Kollam coastal road and is easily accessible from all nearby areas

Food

At Sanskriti home, you are not a guest but you are a family member. You have all traditional Kerala style dishes available cooked under the supervision of caretakers. Fish, prawns, poultry and all vegetarian dishes are on the menu.

Know Your Host

The Homestay belongs to Danesh Antony who is a native of Kochi, Kerala and has a great insight of the locality. He is a working as a principal in renowned Private School in Kollam and his interest towards Tourism Industry led him to open his own Homestay on one of his best propetties in Varkala. He manages homestay along with his brother Ajith and is available at the service of his guests 24*7 upon their arrival. He loves interacting and sharing stories and his culture to its guests and gives them tour of Island by himself. He make sure Guests face no inconvenience, so personally escorts them to the Homestay in his Open Yamaha Boat. The friendly nature of host makes your stay a homely experience.
