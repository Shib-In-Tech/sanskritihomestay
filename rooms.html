About Sanskriti Homestay

Sansriti home plays a pivotal role in promoting beach tourism near the exotic beaches of Kappil and Pozhikkara, in Kollam district, Kerala. With the bluish-green waters of Paravoor lake in the back drop, this is the ideal place to lose yourself in the lap of nature. You can experience living in a traditional home along with all modern facilities and an array of delicacies.

Food

Fresh and delicious Kerala style food is served. Both Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian food is available. There is a small kitchen on the premises where host cook delicacies along with his friend and serve it to their guests. They purchase fresh grocery upon arrival of its guests as per taste and budget of the guest. Parippu, Parppidakam, Sambar, Aviyal, Thoran, Morukachiyathhu, Karimeen Fry, Chippi Kakka are few of the dishes served by the host that are worth a try.

Activities

Bird Watching | Reading | Fishing | Boating | Meditation | Village Walk

Know Your Host

The Homestay belongs to Danesh Antony who is a native of Kochi, Kerala and has a great insight of the locality. He is a working as a principal in renowned Private School in Kollam and his interest towards Tourism Industry led him to open his own Homestay on one of his best propetties in Varkala. He manages homestay along with his brother Ajith and is available at the service of his guests 24*7 upon their arrival. He loves interacting and sharing stories and his culture to its guests and gives them tour of Island by himself. He make sure Guests face no inconvenience, so personally escorts them to the Homestay in his Open Yamaha Boat. The friendly nature of host makes your stay a homely experience.
